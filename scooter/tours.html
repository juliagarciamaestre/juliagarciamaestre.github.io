The Mayan Cacao Company

The perfect place to learn a little more about chocolate history. The Mayan Cacao Company have a restaurant, a souvenier store and a bar where you can enjoy all kinds of chocolates.

Adress:

Costera Sur Av. KM 1500

Chankanaab Natural Reserve

Chankanaab is a protected area where a lot of different animals live. It's the perfect place for people who want to connect with nature.

Adress:

Costera Sur Av. KM 9.5

Opens from Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM
